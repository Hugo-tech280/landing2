Frequently Asked Questions

 

 

What is included in the monthly subscription?

Our monthly subscription covers vehicle rent, insurance, maintenance, summer/winter tires, and road tax.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes, with Turismo Plus and Turismo One, you can pause your subscription at no cost.

How do vehicle switches work?

Turismo Plus allows 4 vehicle switches per year, and Turismo One offers unlimited switches.

What are the activation fees?

Activation fees vary: €750 for Turismo Single, €1980 for Turismo Plus, and €4780 for Turismo One.
